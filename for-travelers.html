Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a bus ticket?

Simply search for your route, select a bus, choose your seat, enter passenger details, and complete payment. You'll receive your ticket instantly via email and SMS.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking through our platform. Refunds are processed according to the operator's cancellation policy, which is displayed at the time of booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and net banking. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Will I receive a confirmation?

Yes, immediately after successful payment, you'll receive a booking confirmation and digital ticket via email and SMS with all your journey details.

What if my bus is delayed or cancelled?

You'll be notified immediately of any delays or cancellations. Our support team will help you reschedule or process a full refund as per your preference.
