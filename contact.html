Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. Urgent operator or technical issues receive priority attention.

I'm a bus operator. How do I get started?

Fill out the contact form selecting "Bus Operator Inquiry" as your subject. Our sales team will reach out to schedule a demo and discuss onboarding.

I need help with my booking. What should I do?

For booking-related queries, select "Traveler Support" in the form above, or email support@bookdito.com with your booking reference number.

Do you offer technical integration support?

Yes! Select "Technical Support" or "Partnership Opportunity" in the form, and our technical team will assist with API integrations and technical requirements.

Can I visit your office in person?

Yes! We welcome office visits. Please email or call your regional office in advance to schedule an appointment.
